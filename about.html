About Aroma Paradise

About Aroma Paradise

Aroma Paradise is a small candle business created with the goal of bringing calm, comfort, and beautiful fragrances into everyday spaces. Each candle is carefully hand-poured using quality fragrance oils and designed to create a relaxing atmosphere whether you're studying, relaxing at home, or spending time with family. We focus on simple but elegant scents that fill the room without being overwhelming.

As a young entrepreneur, this brand represents creativity, learning, and building something from the ground up. Aroma Paradise is more than candles — it's about creating a small business that grows with passion and dedication while delivering products people genuinely enjoy. Every candle is made with attention to detail so customers get a clean burn, long lasting scent, and a product they can trust.

Meet the Founder

My name is Arvin and I’m a 14-year-old student at Windermere High School. I started Aroma Paradise because I wanted to create candles that bring peace, comfort, and amazing aromas to every home. Each candle is carefully hand-poured with love and designed to create a relaxing environment for any space. My goal is to make high quality, affordable candles that everyone can enjoy. This is just the beginning of my candle journey, and I’m excited to grow Aroma Paradise into a brand people trust and love.
