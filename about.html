About Aroma Paradise

Meet the man who started it all

Aroma Paradise was created by a passionate young entrepreneur with a love for creating relaxing environments through scent. My name is the founder of Aroma Paradise and I am a 14 year old student currently attending Windermere High School. I started this candle brand because I’ve always loved how scents can completely change the mood of a room. A calm scent can help you focus while studying, relax after a long day, or simply make your space feel more comfortable. What started as an idea quickly turned into a mission: to create affordable candles that smell amazing and bring peace to people's homes. Every candle from Aroma Paradise is designed to be simple, relaxing, and enjoyable for everyone. At only $10 per candle, the goal is to make high quality scents available for anyone who wants to upgrade their room or home with amazing aromas. This is just the beginning of Aroma Paradise, and there is much more to come.
