About Aroma Paradise

Meet the Founder

My name is Arvin and I’m a 14-year-old student at Windermere High School. I started Aroma Paradise because I wanted to create candles that bring peace, comfort, and amazing aromas to every home. Each candle is carefully hand-poured with love and designed to create a relaxing environment for any space. My goal is to make high quality, affordable candles that everyone can enjoy. This is just the beginning of my candle journey, and I’m excited to grow Aroma Paradise into a brand people trust and love.
