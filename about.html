About Aroma Paradise

Aroma Paradise was created to bring calming, clean fragrances into everyday spaces. Each candle is designed to create an atmosphere that helps people relax, focus, and enjoy their home. From floral scents to fresh citrus aromas, every fragrance is selected to create a peaceful environment.

Meet The Man Who Started It All

My name is Swift and I’m the founder of Aroma Paradise. I’m a 14-year-old student who attends Windermere High School in Florida. I started this brand because I wanted to build something of my own and create products that people enjoy using.

What began as a simple idea quickly became a passion for designing candles that look great and smell amazing. My goal is to keep improving every scent and grow Aroma Paradise into a brand people trust for high quality fragrances.
