About Aroma Paradise

About The Brand

Aroma Paradise is a small candle business created with the goal of bringing relaxing and refreshing scents into everyday spaces. Every candle is designed to fill a room with comforting aromas that help people relax, focus, and enjoy their environment.

The brand focuses on affordable but high quality candles so anyone can enjoy amazing scents without spending a lot. From calming lavender to refreshing eucalyptus, each scent is chosen to create a peaceful atmosphere in homes, bedrooms, or work spaces.

Meet The Founder

My name is Arvin and I am a 14 year old student at Windermere High School. I started Aroma Paradise because I love creating things and wanted to build my own small business. Candles are something that can completely change the feeling of a room, and I wanted to make scents that people enjoy every day.
